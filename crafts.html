9 Fun Halloween Crafts

Easy, kid-friendly projects with simple materials. Click “How-to” to explore tutorials.

Paper Plate Ghosts

  • Paper plate
  • Tissue/streamers
  • Black marker
  • Tape
  1. Draw eyes/mouth on the plate.
  2. Tape tissue strips on the back for the “tail.”
  3. Punch a hole and hang with string.
How-to

Toilet Roll Bats

  • Toilet roll
  • Black paint
  • Paper wings
  • Googly eyes
  1. Paint the roll black; fold top for bat ears.
  2. Cut wing shapes; glue to the back.
  3. Add eyes and draw fangs.
How-to

Mummy Jar Lanterns

  • Glass jar
  • Gauze/tape
  • Googly eyes
  • LED tea light
  1. Wrap jar with gauze; secure with tape.
  2. Stick on googly eyes.
  3. Put an LED candle inside.
How-to

Leaf Rubbing Pumpkins

  • Leaves
  • Paper
  • Crayons
  • Scissors
  1. Place leaf under paper; rub with crayon.
  2. Cut into pumpkin shapes.
  3. Add stems and jack-oʼ-lantern faces.
How-to

Yarn Spider Web Plates

  • Paper plate
  • Hole punch
  • Yarn
  • Paper spider
  1. Punch holes around plate edge.
  2. Weave yarn to form a web.
  3. Add a cut-paper or pom-pom spider.
How-to

Painted Rock Monsters

  • Smooth rocks
  • Acrylic paint
  • Markers
  • Googly eyes
  1. Paint base colors; let dry.
  2. Add eyes, teeth, and scars.
  3. Seal with clear coat (optional).
How-to

Popsicle Stick Haunted House

  • Craft sticks
  • Glue
  • Paint
  • Paper windows
  1. Glue sticks to form a house shape.
  2. Paint dark colors; add roof.
  3. Glue paper windows/door; draw webs.
How-to

Pumpkin Seed Art

  • Clean pumpkin seeds
  • Food coloring
  • Paper
  • Glue
  1. Dye seeds orange/green/purple; dry.
  2. Sketch a simple shape.
  3. Glue seeds to fill the design.
How-to

Witch Hat Bookmarks

  • Black/orange paper
  • Scissors
  • Glue
  • Gold pen
  1. Fold corner bookmark base.
  2. Cut hat/brim; glue to the base.
  3. Add buckle/stars with pen.
